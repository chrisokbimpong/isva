Appropriate security controls to implement for a medical system to prevent exposure of personal information, modification of data or the accessibility of data based on various security controls.

Integrity controls

these controls help mitigate the risk of modification of data by implementing policies or procedures to protect health information from improper or unauthorized alteration or destruction. E.g. identifying all users who have been authorized to access the system, identifying any possible unauthorized sources that may be able to intercept the information and modify it.
